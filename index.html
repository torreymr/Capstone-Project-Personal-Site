Interests

MMA/Fighting

sports_martial_arts

Growing up, I wasn't a big sports fan. I didn't play sports in school and didn't watch them outside of it. Then came UFC 268 near the end of 2021, Usman vs. Covington 2, and since then I haven't missed a night of fights. A lot of things captivate me towards the sport: The build up, the stylistic match ups, the blood shed, the risk and heart it takes, and SO MUCH more.

But to go deeper than the sport itself, I think it can be seen as a reflection of what we call life. We as people get beat up, we suffer, we lose, whether it be physically or mentally. It is our choice to be a fighter, to fight back against the pain, to get back up after we get knocked down, to keep relentlessly moving forward until the final buzzer sounds. In a sense, we are all fighters.
